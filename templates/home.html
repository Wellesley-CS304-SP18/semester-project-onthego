Welcome to On-the-Go, a website where Wellesley students can order food online from El Table. Because Wellesley students are constantly on the move, many people cannot or do not want to wait thirty to forty minutes for their orders to be made. On-the-Go allows you to be somewhere else while your order is being made and to pick it up when it's ready.
